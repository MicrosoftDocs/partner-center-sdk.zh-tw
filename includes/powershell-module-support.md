The [Partner Center PowerShell module](https://github.com/microsoft/partner-center-powershell/) is commonly used by partners to manage their Partner Center resources. It is an open source project maintained by the partner community. Since this module is maintained by the partner community, it is not officially supported by Microsoft. You can [get help from the community](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/partner+center) or [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/microsoft/partner-center-powershell/issues) if you encounter a problem.
